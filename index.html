Bio

Alejandro aka Blessed, was born in music. But it was Summer 2014 in Madrid when he recently got 18 years old that he assisted to a club, Zoologico Club. He really had fun there and decided to take a step further of being a music addict and he downloaded Logic Pro and started making noises. A few weeks later he earned some money as a Software Developer and bought a Numark Mixtrack Pro II and downloaded Traktor to learn how to mix. Febraury 2015 he started playing at a famous club at Madrid (Joy Eslava) and that was his first step as a club-DJ. Alex it's also working hard with his tracks that he will release soon.

Blessed likes to describe himself as an ambitious, crazy and funny guy that loves making people have a good time. He always says the maybe his goals are so ambitious but he will work his cherry off and he won't stop until achieving them

And the best its yet to come...
